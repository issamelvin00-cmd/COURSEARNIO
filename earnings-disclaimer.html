Earnings Disclaimer

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

⚠️ Important Notice

Earnings depend entirely on individual effort. There is no guaranteed income. The referral commissions and earnings potential discussed on this platform are not promises of specific financial results.

1. No Income Guarantees

Earnio makes absolutely no guarantee regarding earnings or income of any kind. Any references to income potential, earnings examples, or financial testimonials are for illustrative purposes only. Your results will depend on:

  • Your individual effort and dedication
  • Your marketing and networking skills
  • The size and quality of your network
  • Market conditions and demand for courses
  • Time invested in promoting the platform
The typical user who does not actively promote courses earns KES 0 in referral commissions.

2. How Our Referral Program Works

Our referral program is a legitimate affiliate marketing system:

  • You earn commissions only when someone you refer purchases a course
  • You do not earn money just for recruiting people
  • There are no fees required to participate in the referral program
  • Commission rates range from 20-40% based on your tier

3. This Is Not a "Get Rich Quick" Scheme

Earnio is an educational platform with a referral program. It is:

  • NOT a pyramid scheme
  • NOT multi-level marketing (MLM)
  • NOT a passive income opportunity
  • NOT guaranteed money

Success requires active effort in promoting courses and building a genuine audience interested in learning.

4. Testimonials & Examples

Any success stories, testimonials, or earnings examples shared on this platform:

  • Represent individual results, not typical outcomes
  • Are not guarantees of future earnings
  • May include exceptional cases that are not representative

5. Educational Value First

The primary purpose of Earnio is to provide quality educational content. The referral program is a secondary feature that rewards users for sharing courses they find valuable. We encourage you to:

  • Focus on learning and personal development first
  • Only recommend courses you genuinely believe are helpful
  • Be honest in your promotions about potential earnings

6. Legal Compliance

Our referral program is designed to comply with consumer protection laws in Kenya and similar markets. Key compliance features:

  • No payment required to join the referral program
  • Earnings tied strictly to product sales, not recruitment
  • Transparent commission rates
  • Clear disclosure of earning potential

7. Your Responsibility

As a referrer, you agree to:

  • Not make false claims about earning potential
  • Clearly disclose your affiliate relationship when promoting
  • Not use deceptive marketing practices
  • Not target vulnerable individuals with unrealistic promises
