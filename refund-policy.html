Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

üí° Quick Summary

Course purchases are refundable within 7 days if you've completed less than 25% of the content. Membership fees are non-refundable. Referral commissions are not affected by course refunds.

1. Course Refunds

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. You may request a refund for any course if:

  • The request is made within 7 days of purchase
  • You have completed less than 25% of the course content
  • You provide a brief reason for the refund request

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Membership/activation fees
  • Courses where more than 25% has been completed
  • Courses purchased more than 7 days ago
  • Bundle purchases (individual course refunds not available)
  • Courses acquired through promotional offers marked as non-refundable

3. Referral Commission Impact

When a refund is processed:

  • The referrer's commission for that sale will be reversed
  • If already withdrawn, the amount will be deducted from future earnings
  • This helps prevent fraudulent referral schemes

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at refunds@earnio.com
  • Include your account email and the course name
  • Briefly explain why you're requesting a refund
  • We will process eligible refunds within 5-7 business days

5. Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method (M-Pesa). Please allow 3-5 business days for the funds to appear in your account after approval.

6. Disputes

If your refund request is denied and you believe this was in error, you may appeal by emailing support@earnio.com with additional details. We will review your case within 10 business days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

For refund inquiries, contact refunds@earnio.com
